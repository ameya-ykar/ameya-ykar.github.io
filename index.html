Hello there! Welcome to my homepage :)

My name is Somang Han (you can call me "So"). I am a Decision Scientist at Bluestem Brands, the parent company to 7 dynamic eCommerce retail brands.

I graduated from St. Olaf College with a major in Mathematics and a minor in Statistics. During my undergraduate studies at St. Olaf College, I was in the Center For Interdisplincary Research team, which is funded by NSF, and worked on Topological Data Analysis with Professor Matthew Wright and Professor Matthew Richey. Also, I joined MN Youth Development Research Group, which is led by Professor Michael C. Rodriguez, and worked with graduate students in the Quantitative Methods in Education program from University of Minnesota.

My research interests lie in the intersection of data science and economical behaviors/human behaviors. I love learning new skills (from modeling techiques to hula hooping) and reading books about human behaviors. Some of my hobbies are working on Kaggle Competitions, alpine skiing, and baking.

I maintain a list of my work under the Work Example tab. You may also check out my Kaggle profile. To get a better insight on me, you can have a look at resume. I blog (although quite less frequently), and feel free to connect with me through LinkedIn.
